On this page, you can see the questions and contest pages from the past Code Hackathon weeks and now, the Rookie Hour challenges. We encourage you to read through the problem sets and practice.

Note: Starting with Code Hackathon Week 3, we will not be publishing solutions for our questions. We believe that trying to solve a question on your own is the best way to learn & improve. The sense of satisfaction that we gain on successfully solving a question and all of its hidden test cases is something that keeps us going. This kind of tenacity is what interviewers look for in freshers: persistence, self-reliance, hard-work, and above all, patience. So pick a time that suits you & stick to it. Practice, practice, practice!
